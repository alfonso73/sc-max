sc-max

port of some supercollider ugens to maxmsp. the focus lies on the noisier ones.

all credits are due to the original authors (James McCartney et alt.). these are merely ports to max/msp and might be helpful. ports by stephen lumenta.

some included externals (new ones might be added): 

sc.brownnoise~
sc.clipnoise~
sc.crackle~
sc.dust~
sc.gendy1-3~
sc.graynoise~
sc.lfclipnoise~
sc.lfnoise0-2~
sc.logistic~
sc.mantissamask~
sc.pinknoise~
sc.whitenoise~
